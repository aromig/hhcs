2014-2015 CTC Transportation Information

August 11, 2014

Dear CTC Parents and Students,

For the 2014-2015 school year, Bus 15 and 26 will pick up and drop off CTC students at LaMendola Elementary in the parking area off of Rosebury Dr.

Parking is available for those students who drive to LaMendola. They need to use Rosebury Dr. to access the parking lot.

Bus #15 will stop at Wayne High School in the afternoon only to drop students off who have activities at Wayne High School. The bus will then proceed to LaMendola.

Titus is another location students can access a bus for CTC. Bus 30 and 44 will pick up and drop off students at that location.

Buses will leave at 7:20 a.m. and will return at 2:55 p.m.

If you have any questions concerning Bus transportation to CTC please call the Transportation Department at 237-6385.70-693

Wayne High School Student Drop-off & Pick-up 2014-15

August 7, 2014

The only entrance into the school is the Student Entrance located at the rear of the school. No other doors into the school will be unlocked for students to enter in the mornings. The Student Entrance is centrally located and designed for the large number of students and for safety and security.

The road between the new softball field and the old gym off of Chambersburg Road is the entrance to the drop-off area. Signs will direct you to turn right into a lot reserved strictly for dropping off students in the morning and waiting for your students at the end of the school day. The drop-off lane is 200 yards long; pull to the curb to discharge your student and then turn left at the end of the drive and exit onto Tomburg Rd. There will be no exit back onto Chambersburg Rd. We will have a guard stationed at the Tomburg Rd. intersection to expedite your leaving the parking lot area.

We would ask parents not to drop off their students in the Chesham/Tomburg area in the mornings because that area is already heavily saturated with traffic leaving Wayne High School. We would also ask that parents make an effort to leave several minutes early. It is extremely difficult to handle 600 cars in that area in a 20 minute time span. The student entrance doors are unlocked at 7 a.m. every school day.

Thanks for your co-operation and patience.
